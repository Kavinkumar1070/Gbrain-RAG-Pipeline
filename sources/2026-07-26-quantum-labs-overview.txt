Quantum Labs — Company Overview

Quantum Labs builds real-time sensor-fusion software for industrial robotics.
The company is based on a small, senior engineering team and has stayed heads-down
on product until now.

Elena Cho is CEO and co-founder. She previously led a perception team at a
self-driving vehicle company for five years before starting Quantum Labs.

Raj Patel is Head of Engineering and the second co-founder. He owns the core
fusion pipeline and reviews every release personally before it ships.

Meridian Capital invested in Quantum Labs' Series B round earlier this year.

The company is currently around 12 people.
