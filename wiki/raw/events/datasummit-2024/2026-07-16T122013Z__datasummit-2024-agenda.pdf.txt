DataSummit 2024 — Speaker Program
San Francisco, CA · October 14-15, 2024
About DataSummit
DataSummit is an annual two-day conference for data and analytics leaders, held each year in San
Francisco, California. The 2024 edition drew over 1,200 attendees across enterprise data teams,
analytics vendors, and investors.
Keynote: Maria Chen, Nexora
Maria Chen, founder of Nexora, delivered the day-two keynote on October 15, 2024, titled 'Analytics
Without the Analyst.' Chen discussed how the Nexora Insight Platform has evolved since its 2022
launch, and previewed forecasting features the company gained through its March 2024 acquisition of
Nexora Analytics.
During the Q&A, Chen confirmed Nexora's new partnership with CloudScale Systems, announced the
previous month, and said the integration would ship to CloudScale customers in early 2025.
