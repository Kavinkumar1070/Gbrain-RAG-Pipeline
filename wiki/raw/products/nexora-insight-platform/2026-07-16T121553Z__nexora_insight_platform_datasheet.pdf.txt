Nexora Insight Platform — Product Datasheet
Overview
The Nexora Insight Platform is the flagship product of Nexora Analytics, an enterprise AI company
founded by Maria Chen in 2021. The platform provides real-time analytics and disruption forecasting for
supply chain and logistics operators.
Architecture
The Nexora Insight Platform combines streaming data ingestion with a hybrid retrieval architecture that
blends vector search, keyword search, and graph traversal. This architecture was designed under the
direction of David Okoro, Chief Technology Officer of Nexora Analytics.
The platform ingests data from over 40 supported logistics data sources, including GPS telemetry
feeds, customs filings, and weather disruption APIs.
Deployment
The Nexora Insight Platform launched publicly in June 2022. A major architecture upgrade, internally
called Project Meridian, shipped in August 2024, adding multi-modal retrieval support for image-based
cargo inspection reports.
Customers
As of January 2025, the Nexora Insight Platform is used by over 120 enterprise customers, including
three Fortune 500 logistics companies. Reference customers include Falcon Freight and Meridian
Shipping Co.
Competitive Landscape
The Nexora Insight Platform competes with products from Fathom Systems and Ledger Grid. Nexora
Analytics' internal positioning emphasizes real-time graph-based retrieval as its key differentiator
against competitors' batch-processing approaches.
