NEXORA ANALYTICS — COMPANY PROFILE

Nexora Analytics is an enterprise AI company headquartered in Austin, Texas, founded in 2021. The company builds real-time data intelligence platforms for logistics and supply chain operators.

Founding and Leadership
Nexora Analytics was founded by Maria Chen, who serves as Chief Executive Officer. Prior to founding Nexora, Maria Chen spent six years as a senior engineering lead at a large cloud infrastructure company. David Okoro joined as co-founder and Chief Technology Officer in 2021, bringing a background in distributed systems and machine learning infrastructure.

Product
The company's flagship product is the Nexora Insight Platform, a real-time analytics and forecasting tool used by supply chain teams to predict disruptions before they occur. The platform combines streaming data ingestion with a hybrid retrieval architecture, allowing customers to query operational data using natural language.

Funding
In March 2023, Nexora Analytics raised a 14 million dollar Series A round led by Meridian Ventures. The round also included participation from Cobalt Partners. In November 2024, the company announced a Series B round of 42 million dollars led by Horizon Capital, bringing total funding to approximately 58 million dollars.

Growth
By the end of 2024, Nexora Analytics reported over 120 enterprise customers, including three Fortune 500 logistics companies. The company grew its headcount from 18 employees in 2021 to over 140 employees by January 2025. Nexora Analytics operates offices in Austin and a smaller engineering hub in Toronto, opened in mid-2024.

Competitors
Nexora Analytics competes primarily with two other vendors in the supply chain intelligence space: Fathom Systems and Ledger Grid. Industry analysts have noted that Nexora's differentiation lies in its real-time graph-based retrieval approach compared to competitors' batch-processing models.
