# Quantum Labs Series B

> Quantum Labs Series B is a Series B deal for Quantum Labs; media coverage says the company completed the round earlier this year with investment from Meridian Capital. Quantum Labs also describes the round as led by Meridian Capital and says it is expanding into customer-specific integration projects, starting with Fenwick Robotics.
