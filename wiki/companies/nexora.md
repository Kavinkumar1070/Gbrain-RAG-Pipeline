# Nexora

## Compiled Truth

Nexora is an Austin-based analytics company founded in January 2021 by Maria Chen, with David Okafor joining shortly after as co-founder and CTO. Its core product, the Nexora Insight Platform, launched publicly in late 2022 to let non-technical teams query business data in plain English and receive charts and summaries. In 2024, Nexora expanded the platform by acquiring Nexora Analytics for its forecasting technology, raised a $28 million Series B led by Sequoia Peak Ventures, and partnered with CloudScale Systems for native warehouse integration. The company also operates a smaller engineering office in Toronto that opened in 2023.

## Facts

- Nexora was founded in Austin, Texas in January 2021.
- Maria Chen founded Nexora.
- Maria Chen was a former machine learning lead at a large cloud provider before founding Nexora.
- Maria Chen started Nexora because she was frustrated by how difficult it was for mid-sized businesses to get usable analytics from their own data without hiring a full data team.
- David Okafor joined Nexora as co-founder and Chief Technology Officer in April 2021.
- David Okafor had previously spent six years building distributed systems.
- David Okafor leads Nexora's engineering organization.
- Nexora's flagship product is the Nexora Insight Platform.
- The first public version of the Nexora Insight Platform shipped in late 2022.
- The Nexora Insight Platform lets non-technical teams query business data in plain English.
- The Nexora Insight Platform returns charts and summaries instead of raw tables.
- Nexora acquired Nexora Analytics in March 2024.
- Nexora Analytics had built a forecasting engine.
- Nexora integrated Nexora Analytics' forecasting technology into the Nexora Insight Platform rather than keeping it as a separate product.
- Nexora is headquartered in Austin, Texas.
- Nexora opened a smaller engineering office in Toronto in 2023.
- Nexora raised a $28 million Series B in June 2024.
- Sequoia Peak Ventures led Nexora's June 2024 Series B round.
- Sequoia Peak Ventures had also participated in Nexora's 2022 seed round.
- Nexora announced a partnership with CloudScale Systems in September 2024.
- The partnership with CloudScale Systems offers the Nexora Insight Platform as a native integration inside CloudScale Systems' data warehouse product.
