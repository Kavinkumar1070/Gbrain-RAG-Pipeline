# Nexora Analytics

## Compiled Truth

Nexora Analytics is an Austin-based enterprise AI company founded in 2021 that focuses on real-time data intelligence for logistics and supply chain operators. The profile highlights its leadership team, flagship Nexora Insight Platform, and its use of streaming data and hybrid retrieval for natural-language access to operational data. It also emphasizes the company's growth, including Series A and Series B funding, expanding customer traction, headcount growth, and a Toronto engineering hub. The document positions Nexora against Fathom Systems and Ledger Grid, noting analyst commentary on Nexora's graph-based real-time retrieval approach as a differentiator.

## Facts

- Nexora Analytics is an enterprise AI company headquartered in Austin, Texas.
- Nexora Analytics was founded in 2021.
- Nexora Analytics builds real-time data intelligence platforms for logistics and supply chain operators.
- Maria Chen founded Nexora Analytics.
- Maria Chen serves as Chief Executive Officer of Nexora Analytics.
- Before founding Nexora Analytics, Maria Chen spent six years as a senior engineering lead at a large cloud infrastructure company.
- David Okoro joined Nexora Analytics as co-founder in 2021.
- David Okoro serves as Chief Technology Officer of Nexora Analytics.
- David Okoro has a background in distributed systems and machine learning infrastructure.
- The flagship product of Nexora Analytics is the Nexora Insight Platform.
- The Nexora Insight Platform is a real-time analytics and forecasting tool.
- The Nexora Insight Platform is used by supply chain teams to predict disruptions before they occur.
- The Nexora Insight Platform combines streaming data ingestion with a hybrid retrieval architecture.
- The Nexora Insight Platform allows customers to query operational data using natural language.
- In March 2023, Nexora Analytics raised a 14 million dollar Series A round.
- Meridian Ventures led Nexora Analytics's March 2023 Series A round.
- Cobalt Partners participated in Nexora Analytics's March 2023 Series A round.
- In November 2024, Nexora Analytics announced a 42 million dollar Series B round.
- Horizon Capital led Nexora Analytics's November 2024 Series B round.
- Nexora Analytics said the November 2024 Series B round brought its total funding to approximately 58 million dollars.
- By the end of 2024, Nexora Analytics reported over 120 enterprise customers.
- By the end of 2024, Nexora Analytics reported that three Fortune 500 logistics companies were among its customers.
- Nexora Analytics grew its headcount from 18 employees in 2021 to over 140 employees by January 2025.
- Nexora Analytics operates offices in Austin and Toronto.
- Nexora Analytics opened a smaller engineering hub in Toronto in mid-2024.
- Nexora Analytics competes primarily with Fathom Systems and Ledger Grid.
- Industry analysts have noted that Nexora Analytics is differentiated by its real-time graph-based retrieval approach.
- Industry analysts have contrasted Nexora Analytics's real-time graph-based retrieval approach with competitors' batch-processing models.
