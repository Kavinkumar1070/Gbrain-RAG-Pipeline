# Nexora Analytics

## Compiled Truth

Nexora Analytics is an Austin-based enterprise AI company founded in 2021 by Maria Chen, with David Okoro as co-founder and CTO. The company focuses on real-time data intelligence for logistics and supply chain operators, centered on its Nexora Insight Platform for analytics, forecasting, and natural-language querying of operational data. The profile highlights strong growth, including a March 2023 Series A, a November 2024 Series B bringing total funding to about $58 million, more than 120 enterprise customers by the end of 2024, and headcount growth to over 140 by January 2025. It also positions Nexora against Fathom Systems and Ledger Grid, emphasizing its real-time graph-based retrieval approach as a key differentiator.
