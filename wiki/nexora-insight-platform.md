# Nexora Insight Platform

## Compiled Truth

The document is a product datasheet for Nexora Insight Platform, Nexora Analytics' flagship supply-chain AI product. It describes the platform's architecture as a streaming ingestion system with hybrid retrieval across vector search, keyword search, and graph traversal, and notes that David Okoro led its design. It also outlines the product's market status: public launch in June 2022, a major August 2024 upgrade called Project Meridian that added multimodal retrieval for cargo inspection imagery, and adoption by more than 120 enterprise customers as of January 2025.

- Nexora Analytics is an enterprise AI company.
- Maria Chen founded Nexora Analytics in 2021.

- Nexora Insight Platform provides disruption forecasting for supply chain and logistics operators.
