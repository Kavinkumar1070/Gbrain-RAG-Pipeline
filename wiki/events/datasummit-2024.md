# DataSummit 2024

## Compiled Truth

DataSummit 2024 was a two-day conference in San Francisco held on October 14-15, 2024, drawing more than 1,200 attendees from enterprise data teams, vendors, and investors. A central program highlight was Maria Chen of Nexora, who delivered the October 15 keynote "Analytics Without the Analyst" and discussed the evolution of the Nexora Insight Platform, including forecasting capabilities tied to Nexora's March 2024 acquisition of Nexora Analytics. The event also featured CloudScale Systems and Nexora-led sessions, and all programming took place at the Moscone Center, which has hosted DataSummit since 2021.
